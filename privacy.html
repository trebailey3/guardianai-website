Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GuardianAI collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the GuardianAI mobile application, website, and related services.

GuardianAI is built with a privacy-first approach. We aim to collect only the information needed to operate the app, analyze suspicious content, prevent abuse, provide subscriptions, improve safety features, and support users.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use GuardianAI, you may voluntarily submit suspicious text, emails, links, QR code results, phone numbers, or other content for analysis. You may also contact us directly and provide your name, email address, or other support-related information.

Scan and Analysis Content

GuardianAI may process the content you submit so the app can provide a scam risk assessment, confidence score, explanation, safety tips, and related scam indicators. You should avoid submitting sensitive personal information unless necessary for analysis.

App Usage and Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, usage events, subscription status, and basic diagnostics to keep GuardianAI working properly and improve reliability.

Location Information

If you use location-based features such as the Threat Map or scams near you, GuardianAI may request access to location information. Location access is optional and controlled by your device settings.

Purchase and Subscription Information

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. GuardianAI may receive subscription status, product identifiers, transaction tokens, and related purchase verification information. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Analyze suspicious content and provide scam risk results.
  • Show risk level, confidence score, scam indicators, and safety tips.
  • Operate app features such as History, Threat Map, Ask GuardianAI, Learning Center, News, and Guardian Games.
  • Verify subscription access and restore purchases.
  • Improve app performance, security, reliability, and user experience.
  • Detect abuse, troubleshoot crashes, and protect the service.
  • Respond to support requests and user questions.

3. Screenshots Blocked

To protect your privacy, screenshots may be blocked or limited in certain areas of GuardianAI where sensitive scam analysis or account-related information may appear.

4. History and Saved Results

GuardianAI may allow you to save or view past scan results in History. History is intended to help you review prior scam checks and share helpful warnings with family or friends. You may delete saved results where the app provides that option.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only as needed to operate GuardianAI, including with trusted service providers that help provide hosting, analytics, artificial intelligence analysis, crash reporting, subscription verification, security, or customer support.
